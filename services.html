Services

Care shaped around development, relationships, and real life.

Therapy is never one-size-fits-all. We begin with who you are, what you have lived through, and what support would feel most meaningful now.

Who I Support

Support that meets you where you are.

Each season of life asks for a different kind of care. My work is tailored to development, relationships, nervous-system needs, and the goals that matter most to you.

01

Children & Families

Play is a child’s first language. Through developmentally attuned, attachment-focused care, I help children express emotions, process difficult experiences, build confidence, and strengthen regulation.

Parents and caregivers are included as important partners because healing grows within relationships.

02

Parents & Caregivers

Parent support offers a compassionate way to understand behavior through the lenses of attachment, development, and the nervous system.

The goal is not perfect parenting. It is greater clarity, connection, repair, confidence, and steadiness in the moments that matter.

03

Adolescents & Adults

I support adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, trauma, grief, identity, relationships, and life transitions.

Together, we move beyond survival toward deeper self-understanding, nervous-system flexibility, and a life that reflects your values.

How We May Work Together

Integrated care, chosen with intention.

Modalities are tools—not formulas. We will draw from the approaches that best fit your story, goals, pace, and developmental needs.

01

EMDR

Trauma processing that helps the brain and body integrate painful experiences without requiring you to remain stuck inside them.

02

Play Therapy

Developmentally responsive therapy using play, creativity, and relationship to help children communicate and heal.

03

Parent Coaching

Practical, attachment-focused support for understanding behavior, strengthening connection, setting limits, and repairing after hard moments.

04

IFS-Informed Therapy

A compassionate approach to understanding protective parts, reducing inner conflict, and building a more connected relationship with yourself.

05

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy

Support for relating differently to difficult thoughts and emotions while moving toward the values that matter most.

06

Somatic & Polyvagal-Informed Care

Practices that build awareness of nervous-system patterns and expand your capacity for regulation, connection, and choice.

07

Theraplay® & TraumaPlay™

Relational and play-based approaches supporting safety, attachment, regulation, and trauma recovery for children and families.

08

Interpersonal Neurobiology

Brain- and relationship-informed care that helps make sense of how experiences shape emotion, behavior, connection, and healing.
