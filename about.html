About Jessica

Thoughtful, practical, deeply human therapy.

I am a licensed professional counselor and child and family therapist. My work balances warmth, playfulness, structure, and evidence-informed care.

I help children, adolescents, adults, parents, and families understand what is happening beneath the surface and build steadier ways of responding.

How I work

Structured when structure helps. Spacious when space is needed.

With children, I use play as a natural language for expression and relationship. With parents, I offer practical tools grounded in attachment, development, and regulation. With adolescents and adults, I integrate cognitive, behavioral, somatic, parts-based, and trauma-focused approaches.
