A place to come back home

You do not have to carry everything alone.

Life can leave us feeling overwhelmed, disconnected, reactive, or unsure of what comes next. Therapy creates room to understand your patterns with compassion, build practical tools, and move toward the life and relationships that matter most to you.

At Anchored Inside, the work is warm, collaborative, and grounded in the belief that healing is not about becoming someone new. It is about returning to yourself.

The Anchored Inside approach

Slow. Soft. Sturdy.

Healing is supported when people feel safe enough to be curious, connected enough to take risks, and grounded enough to stay present with what matters.

Integrative and individualized

Practical tools with space for deeper healing.

Therapy may draw from EMDR, IFS and parts work, DBT, CBT, somatic and polyvagal practices, play therapy, Theraplay, and attachment-focused family work. The approach is adapted to your needs rather than asking you to fit into a single method.
